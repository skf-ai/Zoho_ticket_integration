# Login credentials not received

Credentials are emailed to the address given at enrolment, normally within 24
hours of the enrolment being processed.

## Student says they never received credentials

1. Ask them to check the spam, junk, and promotions folders.
2. Ask them to search their inbox for "welcome", "LMS", "Moodle", or
   "lmsadmin@srisiddhanta.org" rather than scrolling — the message is easy to miss.
3. Confirm which email address they registered with. Credentials very often go to
   an address the student has forgotten about, or one with a typo.
4. If enrolment was completed less than 24 hours ago, ask them to wait — the
   email may still be queued.

If it has been more than 24 hours since enrolment, or the registered email
address is wrong, raise a ticket. Only the LMS admin can resend credentials or
correct the email address on the account. Include the student's name and the
email address they expected the credentials at.

## Student registered with the wrong email or phone number

This always needs the LMS admin. Raise a ticket and include both the incorrect
detail on file and the correct one the student is giving you.
